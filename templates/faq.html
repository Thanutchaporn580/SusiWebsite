Frequently Asked Questions

To register an account, click on the "Register" link in the navigation bar and fill in the required information.

After registering, you can log in using your username and password. Click on the "Login" link in the navigation bar and enter your credentials.

Once logged in, you can create a diary entry by clicking on the "Create Note" link. Fill in the title, description, and tags for your entry, then click "Submit".

You can view all your notes by clicking on the "Note" link in the navigation bar. Each note will display its title, description, tags, and the last update date.

To update or delete a note, click on the note you want to modify. You will see options to update or delete the note.

If you have any questions or need support, visit the "Contact" page for our contact information.

To upload a file, click on the "Upload" link in the navigation bar. Select the file you want to upload and click "Submit".

You can view all uploaded images by clicking on the "Images" link in the navigation bar.

To update your profile, click on the "Profile" link in the navigation bar. You can update your profile picture and other information.

To log out, click on the "Logout" link in the navigation bar.
